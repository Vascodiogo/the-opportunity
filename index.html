How it works

Simple for subscribers.
Powerful for merchants.

Subscribers pay with their credit card as usual. The rest happens automatically.

01 —

Pay by card

Subscriber enters their credit card. USDC is purchased automatically and deposited into their self-custodied vault.

02 —

Authorize once

A one-time smart contract permission is set. This is the only action the subscriber ever takes.

03 —

Payments flow

AuthOnce executes payments automatically on schedule. Weekly, monthly, or yearly — the subscriber does nothing.

04 —

Merchant settles

Merchant receives USDC instantly, or converts to euros via a licensed partner. Funds arrive in their bank account.
