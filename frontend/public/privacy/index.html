Privacy Policy

Effective: May 2, 2026 | AuthOnce

1. What we collect

AuthOnce collects only what is necessary to operate your subscription: your email address, payment method reference, and subscription status. We do not sell personal data.

2. How we use it

Your data is used to process payments, send payment notifications, and provide access to your subscriber portal at authonce.io/my-subscriptions.

3. Data storage

Data is stored on secured servers. Wallet private keys for fiat subscribers are encrypted with AES-256-GCM. We never store raw card numbers.

4. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing support@authonce.io. Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time at authonce.io/my-subscriptions.
